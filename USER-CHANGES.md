# What's New in the Sutter SPED Referrals System
## Updated March 6, 2025

We've made several improvements to make the referral process easier and more accurate for you!

---


### **Updated Placement Type Field**
- The placement type option now says **"DHH Interim"** instead of "FRA placement"
- This is just one field on the referral form - the program name stays the same
- Everything else works exactly the same way

### **Easier Form Layout**
- **Better Organization**: Related fields are now grouped together so you don't have to scroll as much
- **3-Column Layout**: More information fits on your screen at once
- **Smarter Field Placement**: Fields that go together are now side-by-side

---

## 📝 **Form Improvements**

### **English Learner Section**
When you mark a student as an English Learner, you'll now see:
- ✅ **"When they became an English learner"** - Add the date this started
- ✅ **"Reclassified?"** - Yes/No question about reclassification status  
- ✅ **"Reclassification Date"** - If yes, when were they reclassified?

*These fields only appear when needed, keeping the form clean.*

### **Special Education Services**
For each service you add:
- ✅ **Individual or Group?** - Now you can specify if the service is one-on-one or in a group
- ✅ **Duration Helper** - The duration field now shows "days" to remind you what to enter
- ✅ **Better Layout** - Service details are organized in a cleaner 3-column format

### **Disability Information**
- ✅ **Added "Tertiary" Option** - Now you can mark disabilities as Primary, Secondary, or Tertiary

---

## 🗂️ **What We Removed**

### **No More "Silo" Field**
- Removed the "Silo (Organizational Grouping)" field
- **Saves you time!** One less field to fill out
- Removed **"Interim Placement to be Reviewed"** from the Special Education Dates section

### **Grade-Based Document Upload Rules**
- **Preschool**: Home Language Survey is hidden
- **TK through 8th**: Home Language Survey is shown and required; Transcripts are hidden
- **9th through 12th**: Home Language Survey and Transcripts are both shown and required
- The **Interim Placement Form upload** remains removed because the packet is completed digitally

## 📧 **New Email Notifications**

You'll now automatically receive email notifications for important updates!

### **For Order Submissions**
- **Requestors**: Get a confirmation email when you submit an order
- **Requestors**: Receive updates when your order status changes
- **SPED Staff**: Get notified when new orders are submitted

### **For Referral Submissions**
- **Submitters**: Receive a confirmation email with your **Confirmation Number** prominently displayed
- **Submitters**: Get notified when your referral status changes
- **SPED Staff**: Get notified when new referrals are submitted and when they're updated

### **Email Features**
- ✅ **Professional Design** - Emails match the SCSOS website design
- ✅ **Clear Information** - All important details included with status badges and links
- ✅ **Confirmation Numbers** - Easy to find and save for later reference
- ✅ **Quick Links** - Direct links to view orders or check referral status

### **Admin Settings**
- **Settings Page**: Admins can configure who receives email notifications
- **Multiple Recipients**: Add multiple email addresses for each notification type
- **Easy Management**: Simple tag-style interface to add/remove email addresses
