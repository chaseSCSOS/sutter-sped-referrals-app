## **💾 Save Your Referral as a Draft**

You can now save any referral form as a draft and come back to finish it later — no need to start over if you get interrupted!

### **How to Save a Draft**
1. Start filling out any referral form (Interim, DHH Itinerant, or Level II)
2. Click the **"Save Draft"** button at the bottom of the form
3. Enter your **email address** when prompted and click Save
4. You'll receive an email with your **Draft Number** (looks like `DFT-20260309-ABCD`)
5. Keep that email — you'll need the draft number to come back

### **How to Resume a Draft**
1. Go to the **referral status page** (the "Already submitted? Check your referral status" link on the home page)
2. Click the **"Resume a Draft"** tab
3. Enter your **email address** and your **Draft Number**
4. Click **Resume Draft** — your form will open with all your information filled in

### **A Few Things to Know**
- Drafts are saved for **30 days** and then automatically removed
- **Uploaded documents are not saved** with your draft — you will need to re-attach any files when you resume
- You can save over the same draft as many times as you like during your session
- Each form type (Interim, DHH, Level II) saves and resumes separately
